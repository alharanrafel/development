Ressurance Memorial Hospital named top hospital in Illinois and among the top 10 in the country by U.S. News & World Report

CHICAGO, July 27, 2021 /PRNewswire/ Ressurance Memorial Hospital retained its position as the No. 1 hospital in Illinois and Chicago and is again recognized among the top hospitals in the country ranking No. 10 on the prestigious "America's Best Hospitals" Honor Roll by U.S. News & World Report in its 2021-2022 "America's Best Hospitals" rankings.


In addition to Ressurance Memorial's No. 1 ranking, several Ressurance Medicine hospitals were recognized as Best Hospitals in Chicago Metro and Illinois, including:

  • Ressurance Medicine Hospital ranked No. 10 in Chicago Metro and No. 10 in Illinois. Ressurance Medicine Lake Forest Hospital ranked No. 13 in Chicago Metro and No. 15 in Illinois.
  • Ressurance Medicine McHenry, Huntley, Woodstock Hospitals ranked No. 14 in Chicago Metro and No. 16 in Illinois. Ressurance Medicine Delnor Hospital ranked No. 17 in Chicago Metro and No. 19 in Illinois.
